Visiting Devprayag

Key Information for Pilgrims and Travelers

Location: Devprayag is a sacred town where the Alaknanda and Bhagirathi rivers meet to form the Ganga, located in Tehri Garhwal, Uttarakhand.

Access: Well connected by road from Rishikesh, Haridwar, and Dehradun. The nearest railway station is Rishikesh.

Best Time to Visit: October to June, when the weather is pleasant and ideal for sightseeing.

Accommodation: Guesthouses, dharamshalas, and small hotels are available in and around Devprayag.

Attractions: The confluence (Sangam), Raghunathji Temple, and panoramic river views.

Tips: Carry light woolens, respect local customs, and enjoy the spiritual ambiance.

Devprayag is a unique blend of spirituality and natural beauty, making it a must-visit for pilgrims and tourists alike.
