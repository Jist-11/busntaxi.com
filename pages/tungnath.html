Visiting Tungnath

Key Information for Pilgrims and Trekkers

Location: Tungnath is the highest Shiva temple in the world, located at an altitude of 3,680 meters in the Rudraprayag district of Uttarakhand.

Access: The trek to Tungnath starts from Chopta and is about 3.5 km long, offering breathtaking views of the Himalayas.

Best Time to Visit: April to November, when the temple is open and the weather is pleasant.

Accommodation: Basic guesthouses and camps are available in Chopta. Limited facilities near the temple.

Attractions: The ancient Tungnath Temple, Chandrashila summit trek, and panoramic mountain vistas.

Tips: Carry warm clothing, start the trek early, and be prepared for sudden weather changes.

Tungnath is a must-visit for devotees and nature lovers, combining spiritual significance with natural beauty.
