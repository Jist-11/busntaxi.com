Visiting Rishikesh

Key Information for Travelers and Pilgrims

Location: Rishikesh is a renowned spiritual town on the banks of the Ganga, known as the Yoga Capital of the World.

Access: Well connected by road and rail from Haridwar, Dehradun, and Delhi.

Best Time to Visit: September to April, when the weather is pleasant and ideal for yoga, rafting, and sightseeing.

Accommodation: Wide range of ashrams, hotels, and guesthouses for all budgets.

Attractions: Laxman Jhula, Ram Jhula, Triveni Ghat, Beatles Ashram, yoga centers, and adventure sports.

Tips: Attend the evening Ganga Aarti, try river rafting, and explore local cafes.

Rishikesh is a vibrant destination for spirituality, adventure, and wellness seekers from around the world.
