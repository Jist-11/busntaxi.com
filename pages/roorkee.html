Visiting Roorkee

Key Information for Travelers and Students

Location: Roorkee is a historic town in Uttarakhand, known for the prestigious IIT Roorkee and the Ganga Canal.

Access: Well connected by road and rail from Delhi, Haridwar, and Dehradun.

Best Time to Visit: October to March, when the weather is pleasant and ideal for sightseeing.

Accommodation: Hotels, guesthouses, and student hostels are available for all budgets.

Attractions: IIT Roorkee campus, Solani Aqueduct, Ganga Canal, and local markets.

Tips: Visit the IIT campus, explore the canal area, and try local snacks.

Roorkee is a blend of academic excellence, history, and local culture, making it a unique stop in Uttarakhand.
