Visiting Haridwar

Key Information for Pilgrims and Travelers

Location: Haridwar is a major pilgrimage city on the banks of the Ganga in Uttarakhand, famous for its ghats and spiritual ambiance.

Access: Well connected by road and rail from Delhi, Dehradun, and other major cities.

Best Time to Visit: October to April, when the weather is pleasant and festivals like Kumbh Mela and Ganga Dussehra are celebrated.

Accommodation: Wide range of hotels, guesthouses, and dharamshalas available for all budgets.

Attractions: Har Ki Pauri ghat, Ganga Aarti, Mansa Devi Temple, Chandi Devi Temple, and local markets.

Tips: Attend the evening Ganga Aarti, respect local customs, and try local vegetarian cuisine.

Haridwar is a spiritual gateway to the Himalayas and a must-visit for devotees and tourists alike.
